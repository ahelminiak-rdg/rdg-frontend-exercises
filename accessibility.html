Accessibility

Web accessibility is often overlooked but vital to a well designed site. There are different ways that people with disabilities need to interact with sites, whether it be high enough contrast between text and images or needing to key through the site without a mouse or have a screen reader digest and read the content to them. The bread and butter of accessibility that cover the gamut of accessibility issues is HTML and styling. Edit the HTML of the page below for proper semantics, then style for accessibility, thinking about things like color contrast and state change affects.
